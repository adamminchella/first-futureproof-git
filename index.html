Below is a selection of common houseplants with some information and care requirements. For information about cacti and succulents please use the links at the top of the page! Generally, houseplants that are not cacti or succulents can tolerate more water and less sun.

Golden Pothos

Latin name

Epipremnum aureum

Water requirements

Keep soil moist. Water when top two inches of soil is dry

Light requirements

Will do best in indirect bright light but can tolerate shade

Propagation

Stem cuttings, best chance of success in Spring/Summer

Soil

Plant will do best in a well draining soil

Repotting

Repot when rootbound, around every 2-3 years

Fertilization

Fertilize once a month in the growing season

Swiss cheese plant

Latin name

Monstera deliciosa

Water requirements

Keep soil moist. Water when top two inches of soil is dry

Light requirements

Bright indirect light

Propagation

Stem cuttings, best chance of success in Spring/Summer

Soil

Plant will do best in a well draining soil

Repotting

Repot when rootbound, around every 2-3 years

Fertilization

Fertilize once a month in the growing season
