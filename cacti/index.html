Cacti are some of the most drought tolerant plants on the planet, storing water in their fleshy bodies. Since they exist mostly in hot, dry environments, many species of cacti have developed spines (prickles) as a defense mechanism to discourage animals.

Angel's Wings

Latin name

Opuntia microdasys

Water requirements

Water infrequently only when the soil has completely dried out.

Light requirements

Will do best in full sun but can tolerate partial sun

Propagation

Cuttings

Soil

Plant will do best in a well draining, gritty soil. Do not let the plant sit in water.

Repotting

Repot when rootbound, around every 2-3 years

Fertilization

Fertilize once a month in the growing season
